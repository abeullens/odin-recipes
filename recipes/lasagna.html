Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a type of pasta made of very wide, flat sheets. It refers to an Italian dish made of stacked layers of lasagna alternating with fillings such ground meats and tomato sauce, béchamel sauce, vegetables, cheeses, and seasonings and spices.The dish may be topped with grated cheese, which becomes melted during baking. Typically cooked pasta is assembled with the other ingredients and then baked in an oven. The resulting baked pasta is cut into single-serving square or rectangular portions.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna sheets
  • 1-1/4 pounds bulk Italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 cans of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans tomato paste
  • 2/3 cup water
  • 2 to 3 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/4 cup minced fresh parsley
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1large egg
  • 4 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 carton of ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Cook noodles according to package directions; Meanwhile cook sausage, beef and onion over medium heat 8-10 minutes. Add garlic during last minute.
  2. Stir in tomatoes, tomato paste, water, sugar, 3 tablespoons parsley, basil, 1/2 teaspoon salt and pepper; let simmer for 30 minutes,
  3. Mix egg, ricotta cheese and remaining 1/4 cup parsley and 1/4 teaspoon salt
  4. Preheat oven to 375°. Spread 2 cups meat sauce into your baking dish. Layer with noodles and a third of the ricotta mixture. Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese and Parmesan cheese. Repeat until dish is nearly full and then top with remaining meat sauce and cheeses
  5. Bake for 50 minutes. Let stand 15 minutes before serving
